## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Smart contract bug or exploit  
**Response:** Immediate transparency, pause operations, communicate fix timeline

**Issue:** User loses funds due to error  
**Response:** Investigate thoroughly, provide support, implement safeguards

**Issue:** Negative press or FUD  
**Response:** Address with facts, provide context, engage respectfully

**Issue:** Regulatory concerns  
**Response:** Consult legal team, communicate compliance efforts, be proactive

### Response Protocol
1. Acknowledge within 1 hour
2. Investigate and gather facts
3. Craft official response
4. Post across all channels
5. Monitor and follow up
6. Document for future reference
