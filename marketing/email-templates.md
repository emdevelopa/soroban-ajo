## Email Design Guidelines

### Brand Voice
- Warm and personal, not corporate
- Clear and concise
- Action-oriented
- Culturally respectful
- Empowering and optimistic

### Technical Specifications
- **Width:** 600px maximum
- **Font:** Web-safe fonts (Arial, Helvetica, Georgia)
- **Colors:** Brand palette (see social media guide)
- **Images:** Optimized, with alt text
- **CTA Buttons:** High contrast, minimum 44x44px
- **Mobile:** Responsive design required

### Best Practices
- Subject line: 40-50 characters
- Preheader text: 85-100 characters
- Personalization: Use first name
- Single primary CTA
- Clear unsubscribe link
- Test across email clients

## Email Metrics & Goals

### Key Metrics to Track

**Open Rate:**
- Industry average: 20-25%
- Target: 30%+

**Click-Through Rate:**
- Industry average: 2-3%
- Target: 5%+

**Conversion Rate:**
- Target: 10%+ (varies by email type)

**Unsubscribe Rate:**
- Target: <0.5%

### A/B Testing Ideas

**Subject Lines:**
- Emoji vs. no emoji
- Question vs. statement
- Personalization vs. generic
- Length (short vs. long)

**Send Times:**
- Morning vs. afternoon vs. evening
- Weekday vs. weekend
- Time zones

**Content:**
- Long-form vs. short-form
- Image-heavy vs. text-heavy
- Single CTA vs. multiple CTAs
- Formal vs. casual tone

**CTAs:**
- Button color
- Button text
- Button placement
- Number of CTAs

## Compliance & Best Practices

### Legal Requirements

‚úÖ Include physical mailing address  
‚úÖ Clear unsubscribe link  
‚úÖ Honor unsubscribe requests within 10 days  
‚úÖ Don't buy email lists  
‚úÖ Get explicit consent (double opt-in recommended)  
‚úÖ Include company name  
‚úÖ Comply with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL

### Deliverability Best Practices

‚úÖ Use authenticated domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)  
‚úÖ Maintain clean email list  
‚úÖ Remove bounces and inactive users  
‚úÖ Avoid spam trigger words  
‚úÖ Balance text and images  
‚úÖ Test before sending  
‚úÖ Monitor sender reputation

### Accessibility

‚úÖ Use semantic HTML  
‚úÖ Include alt text for images  
‚úÖ Ensure sufficient color contrast  
‚úÖ Use readable font sizes (14px minimum)  
‚úÖ Provide plain text version  
‚úÖ Test with screen readers

## Tools & Platforms

### Email Service Providers (ESPs)

**Recommended:**
- **Mailchimp** - User-friendly, good for beginners
- **SendGrid** - Developer-friendly, great deliverability
- **ConvertKit** - Creator-focused, excellent automation
- **Customer.io** - Advanced segmentation and triggers

**Budget:** $50-200/month depending on list size

### Design Tools

- **Stripo** - Email template builder
- **BEE Free** - Drag-and-drop editor
- **Litmus** - Email testing across clients
- **Email on Acid** - Testing and analytics

### Analytics

- **Google Analytics** - Track email traffic
- **ESP built-in analytics** - Open, click, conversion rates
- **Mixpanel** - Advanced user behavior tracking
