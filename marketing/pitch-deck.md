## Presentation Tips

### Before the Pitch

**Preparation:**
- [ ] Rehearse 10+ times
- [ ] Time yourself (aim for 15-20 minutes)
- [ ] Prepare for common questions
- [ ] Research your audience
- [ ] Test all technology
- [ ] Print backup copies
- [ ] Prepare leave-behind materials

**Materials to Bring:**
- Laptop with presentation
- Backup on USB drive
- Printed deck (2 copies)
- Business cards
- One-pager
- Demo on phone/tablet

### During the Pitch

**Do:**
‚úÖ Make eye contact  
‚úÖ Show passion and enthusiasm  
‚úÖ Tell stories, not just facts  
‚úÖ Pause for questions  
‚úÖ Be honest about challenges  
‚úÖ Listen actively  
‚úÖ Take notes on feedback  

**Don't:**
‚ùå Read from slides  
‚ùå Rush through content  
‚ùå Get defensive about questions  
‚ùå Oversell or exaggerate  
‚ùå Use too much jargon  
‚ùå Go over time  
‚ùå Forget to ask for the investment  

### After the Pitch

**Follow-Up:**
- Send thank you email within 24 hours
- Provide requested materials promptly
- Answer questions thoroughly
- Keep them updated on progress
- Be responsive and professional

**Materials to Send:**
- Pitch deck (PDF)
- Executive summary
- Financial model
- Due diligence folder
- References

## Customization Guide

### For Different Audiences

**Investors (VCs, Angels):**
- Emphasize market size and growth potential
- Focus on financials and unit economics
- Highlight team experience and traction
- Show clear path to returns

**Strategic Partners:**
- Emphasize mutual benefits
- Focus on integration opportunities
- Highlight complementary strengths
- Show user overlap and synergies

**Corporate Development:**
- Emphasize technology and IP
- Focus on acquisition potential
- Highlight strategic fit
- Show defensibility and moat

**Grants and Foundations:**
- Emphasize social impact
- Focus on mission alignment
- Highlight underserved communities
- Show measurable outcomes

### Timing Variations

**2-Minute Elevator Pitch:**
- Slides 1, 2, 4, 7, 20, 23
- Focus on problem, solution, market, ask

**10-Minute Pitch:**
- Slides 1-5, 7-8, 11-12, 17, 20-23
- Core story with key metrics

**20-Minute Pitch:**
- All main slides (1-23)
- Full story with details

**45-Minute Deep Dive:**
- All slides including appendix
- Detailed Q&A
- Live demo

## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style

**Colors:**
- Primary: Stellar Blue (#7B61FF)
- Secondary: Ajo Gold (#FFB800)
- Accent: Deep Purple (#4A148C)
- Neutral: Gray (#424242)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Inter Bold, 36-48pt
- Body: Inter Regular, 18-24pt
- Captions: Inter Medium, 14-16pt

**Layout:**
- Consistent margins (60px)
- Generous white space
- Left-aligned text
- Right-aligned visuals

**Images:**
- High quality (300 DPI)
- Authentic, diverse people
- Relevant to content
- Consistent style

### Slide Design Principles

1. **One Idea Per Slide**
   - Don't overcrowd
   - Focus attention
   - Easy to understand

2. **Visual > Text**
   - Use images and diagrams
   - Minimize bullet points
   - Show, don't tell

3. **Consistency**
   - Same fonts throughout
   - Consistent colors
   - Aligned elements

4. **Contrast**
   - Readable text
   - Clear hierarchy
   - Emphasis on key points
